Module Hangman

module Hangman: sig .. end

Hangman is a relatively simple minigame that asks the player to find an unknown word or phrase

The player has 10 lives and each time they guess a character or word incorrectly, they lose a turn. The player is allowed to guess either a character or word at each turn.
